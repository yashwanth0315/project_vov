About Us

At Voice Of Voiceless, we are dedicated to fostering a harmonious coexistence between humans and the diverse wildlife that shares our campus. Our mission is driven by compassion and a commitment to the well-being of animals and birds in Rourkela.

Tagging

The campus dogs have been tagged with yellow tags for being identified by the campus people

Poonch Parade

The activity is done to spread the happiness of the new year among campus animals and birds

Feeding Points

decides the location of feeding points, where feeding is strictly done once a day by each member of the club

Treatments

Treatment and veterinary check-ups of animals and birds in need of medications are done as advised by the veterinary doctors of Rourkela.

Mission

Educating the general public on how to deal with the diverse wildlife found on campus.Not just to care for the animals on campus, but also to create a safe environment for us humans to co-exist.Take positive actions towards a healthier relationship between 'us' and 'them'

Vision

Establishing a smooth co-existence where both humans and animals live peacefully preserving their rights and practicing their duties
